Spiced Wine

Description

Conditum Paradoxum, also known as spiced wine, was a popular beverage to consume during the roman's celebration of Saturnalia. It was valued enough to be included in one of the oldest cookbooks in human history: the Apicius. This cookbook is what is going to be used as a reference in the creation of this ancient drink. The combination of ingredients should result in a gold full bodied color with a sweet honey taste, with a scent of ginger and pepper.

Ingredients

To make spiced wine at home, you will need:

  • 750 ml of white wine
  • 1 date fruit
  • 1 tsp of black pepper
  • 1 cup of honey
  • 1/2 tsp of fennel seeds
  • 2 bay leaves
  • A pinch of saffron
