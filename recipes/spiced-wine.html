Spiced Wine

Description

Conditum Paradoxum, also known as spiced wine, was a popular beverage to consume during the roman's celebration of Saturnalia. It was valued enough to be included in one of the oldest cookbooks in human history: the Apicius. This cookbook is what is going to be used as a reference in the creation of this ancient beverage. The combination of ingredients should result in a gold, full bodied color with a sweet honey tatse with a scent of ginger and pepper.
