Reuben Sandwich

Description

A rebuen is a grilled sandwich that consists of swiss cheese, corned beef, and rye bread .

Ingredients

The ingredients used for making this sandwich include:

  • 8 slices of rye bread
  • half a cup of island dressing
  • 8 slices of swiss cheese
  • 8 slices of corned beef (can be deli sliced)
  • 1 cup of drained sauerkraut
  • 2 tablespoons of softened butter

Steps to take

The steps to follow in making a reuben are such:

  1. Heat up your griddle/skillet at a medium heat
  2. Spread island dressing on one side of your bread slices
  3. Place one slice of swiss cheese, 2 slices of corned beef, 1/4 cup of sauerkraut, and another slice of swiss cheese on top of one bread slice
  4. Do the previous step another three times
  5. Use the remaining 4 pieces of bread to top off the four unfinished sandwiches, with the dressing side facing down
  6. Add butter to the top of each sandwich
  7. You should end up with 4 sandwiches
  8. Place the sandwiches on the heated griddle butter side down
  9. Butter the tops of the sandwiches
  10. Grill until the bread is golden brown, not more than 4 minutes per side

The sandwiches should be ready to go, eat them while they are hot!
