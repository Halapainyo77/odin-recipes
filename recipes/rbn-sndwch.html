Reuben Sandwich

Description

A rebuen is a grilled sandwich that consists of swiss cheese, corned beef, and rye bread .

Ingredients

The ingredients used for making this sandwich include:

  • 8 slices of rye bread
  • half a cup of island dressing
  • 8 slices of swiss cheese
  • 8 slices of corned beef (can be deli sliced)
  • 1 cup of drained suaerkraut
  • 2 tablespoons of softened butter
