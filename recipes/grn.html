Green Eggs and Ham

Description

"I like green eggs and ham!"

"I do! I like them, Sam-I-am!"

Hopefully you were as convinved as I was by the rhetorical genius of this mythical creature known as Sam-I-am. Sam takes on an epic quest to convince his neighbor to try these supposedly godly eggs and ham, so if that doesn't convince you to try them, what will?

Originating from the 1960 Dr Seuss book, green eggs and ham is a dish that is not limited to the fictional world. One can make their own green eggs and ham, and pester their neighbor about it afterwards

Ingredients

Some ingredients for this dish include:

  • 8 eggs
  • 8 slices of ham
  • 2 cups of baby spinach
  • some salt and pepper

Other Items:

  • Blender
  • Skillet(nonstick) or a griddle
  • Spatula
  • Microwave safe plate

Steps to follow

These are the steps to follow in creating this dish:

  1. Warm up your skillet (can be swapped with a griddle) on medium heat
  2. Put the ham slices on a plate thats microwave safe
  3. Heat ham for 30 seconds, then cover them
  4. Crack your 8 eggs and drop the yolk into a blender
  5. Add your 2 cups of baby spinach, and then have the blender on low speed
  6. Change the blender setting to high speed until everything inside the blender is green
  7. Pour the contents of the blender onto the skillet, using your spatula to scrape the eggs as they cook
  8. Push the eggs to the outside of the skillet pan, while letting the uncooked parts slide to the center
  9. Spinkle salt and pepper onto the eggs while they are still wet
  10. Gently stir and scrape the eggs with your spatula until removing them from the pan
  11. Serve the eggs with the hopefully still warm harm

And there you have it, green eggs and ham by Sam-I-am
